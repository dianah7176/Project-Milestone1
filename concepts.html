Key Concepts

Statelessness
Each request is independent; state is maintained via cookies, sessions, or tokens in applications.
Multiplexing
Multiple concurrent streams over a single connection (HTTP/2 and HTTP/3) to reduce latency.
Head-of-line Blocking
When one delayed item holds up others on the same connection; solved at the transport level by QUIC.
TLS (Transport Layer Security)
Cryptographic protocol providing confidentiality and integrity; basis of HTTPS.
QUIC
UDP-based transport with integrated TLS 1.3 and stream-level recovery; foundation for HTTP/3.
Caching
Re-using responses with controls such as Cache-Control, ETag, and validators.
